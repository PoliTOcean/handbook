# 🏛️ Organization & Divisions

How the **PoliTOcean** team is structured.

## Leadership

- **Team Leader** — overall coordination and representation of the team.
- Each **department** is led by a **Project Manager (PM)** and a
  **Chief Technical Officer (CTO)**.
- Each **division** has a **Head of Division (HoD)** and a
  **Technical Officer of Division (ToD)**.

```
Team Leader
├── MATE Department            (PM + CTO)
├── R&D Department             (PM + CTO)
└── Communication & Marketing  (PM + CTO)
        └── each → Divisions (HoD + ToD + Members)
```

## Departments & divisions

### 🔧 MATE Department
Builds the competition ROV: mechanics, electronics and firmware.

| Division | Focus |
|----------|-------|
| Mechanical | Frame, structure, mechanical subsystems of the ROV |
| Electronic | Power, electronics boards, wiring |
| Firmware | Embedded software running on the ROV |

### 🔬 R&D Department
Research and development on new technologies and subsystems.

| Division | Focus |
|----------|-------|
| Mechanical | Mechanical R&D and prototyping |
| Electronic | Electronics R&D |
| Firmware | Firmware R&D |
| Hydrodynamic | Fluid dynamics, propulsion, hull design |
| Materials | Materials research and testing |

### 📣 Communication & Marketing
Promotes the team and manages relationships with sponsors.

| Division | Focus |
|----------|-------|
| Graphic & Media | Graphic design, photo/video, social media |

> ℹ️ The live, up-to-date org chart (with names and photos) is on the
> [website → About Us](https://politocean.polito.it/about-us).

## Roles glossary

| Acronym | Role |
|---------|------|
| PM | Project Manager — manages a department |
| CTO | Chief Technical Officer — technical lead of a department |
| HoD | Head of Division — leads a division |
| ToD | Technical Officer of Division — technical lead of a division |
