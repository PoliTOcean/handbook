# 🚀 Onboarding

Welcome to **PoliTOcean**! This page walks you through everything you need to
get started as a new member.

> 👋 Lost? Ask your **Head of Division (HoD)** or write in the team chat — no
> question is a stupid question.

## 1. Accounts & access

Make sure you have access to (ask your HoD or the Comm department if you don't):

- [ ] **Team chat** (Slack / Discord / WhatsApp — whichever the team uses)
- [ ] **GitHub** — send your GitHub username to be added to the
  [PoliTOcean org](https://github.com/PoliTOcean) and your division's team
- [ ] **Google Drive / shared storage** — design files, documents, datasheets
- [ ] **Team email** (`politocean@gmail.com`) — only if your role needs it
- [ ] **Lab / workshop access** at the Politecnico (badge / keys)

## 2. Get to know the team

- Read the [Organization & Divisions](organization.md) page to understand how
  the team is structured and where your division fits.
- Read the [Processes & Conventions](processes.md) page **before** your first
  contribution (especially the Git workflow).
- Browse the [GitHub repositories](https://github.com/PoliTOcean) relevant to
  your division.

## 3. Set up your tools

Depending on your division you'll need different software. A few common ones:

| Area | Typical tools |
|------|---------------|
| Software / Firmware | Git, VS Code, the toolchain for your project (ROS 2, PlatformIO, STM32CubeIDE…) |
| Mechanical | CAD (SolidWorks / Fusion 360), shared Drive for parts |
| Electronic | KiCad / Altium, datasheets repository |
| Comm & Marketing | Figma / Adobe suite, social media access |

> See your division's section in [Organization & Divisions](organization.md)
> for the exact stack.

## 4. Your first contribution

1. Pick a small task with your HoD/ToD.
2. Follow the [Git workflow](processes.md#git-workflow): branch → commit → PR.
3. Ask for a review. Iterate. Merge. 🎉

## ✅ Onboarding checklist

- [ ] I'm in the team chat
- [ ] I have GitHub access to my division's repos
- [ ] I have access to the shared Drive
- [ ] I've read Organization & Processes
- [ ] My dev tools are installed
- [ ] I've made my first PR

Welcome aboard! 🌊
